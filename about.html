Our Story

Founded in September 2025 and headquartered in Mumbai, India, Nexaflo Automations began with a simple realization: the world doesn't need more agencies—it needs more solutions.

We initially started as an AI consultancy, building custom solutions for individual businesses. But we quickly recognized the limitations of that model—unpredictable revenue, low barriers to entry, and the rise of no-code platforms commoditizing the space.

That's when we pivoted. Instead of building one-off solutions for clients, we shifted our focus to identifying high-value problems and creating proprietary products that serve entire industries. We build solutions that reduce friction, automate complexity, and deliver measurable value—not just for businesses, but for the people they serve.

Meet the Founder

Devansh Seth

Founder & CEO

Age 21

Since childhood, Devansh has always viewed the world differently. Where others saw routine, he saw opportunities to help. His natural empathy led him to listen to people's problems and seek ways to create value in their lives.

He initially envisioned starting a consultancy to help people and businesses find purpose and solve operational challenges. But he lacked the experience and roadmap to make it happen.

After exploring multiple paths, Devansh discovered his calling: using AI to build real-world solutions that fundamentally change how people work and live. His passion for problem-solving and puzzles now drives Nexaflo's mission—creating automation that doesn't just serve businesses, but empowers the people within them.
