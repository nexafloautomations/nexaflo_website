Requesting Data Deletion

Nexaflo Automations values your privacy and complies with Meta’s data deletion requirements. You may request deletion of your personal data associated with Nexaflo Automations at any time.

  • Email Request: Send a data deletion request by email to nexafloautomations@gmail.com.
    Please include your name, business, contact number, and specify the data/account/service you want removed.
  • Identity Verification: To protect privacy, we may request you reply from your registered email or validate your WhatsApp or business phone number on file.

You will receive a confirmation email about your request status (processing, completed, or reason for denial if it cannot be performed).

Processing Time

Requests are typically processed within 5 business days. If additional verification is required, we will notify you by email or WhatsApp.

Meta/WhatsApp Callback Requests

If you initiate deletion via Meta/WhatsApp platforms, we will receive and honor signed deletion callback requests as required by Meta. You will receive a confirmation upon successful deletion if Meta has provided contact details.
